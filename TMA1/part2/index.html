Welcome Coders!

This is a coding website that teaches you the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript!

HTML is short for HyperText Markup Language.

CSS is short for Cascading StyleSheet.

JS is short for JavaScript.

What the Application Does:

  1. Teaches the fundamentals of HTML/CSS and JavaScript technologies, as well as advanced concepts about them.
  2. Every unit has a quiz at the end to test your knowledge and to be able to apply what you've learned after reading the contents.
  3. You get to keep track of your progress and relearn the topics you had difficulty with!

What You Get From It:

By the end of this course, you will be able to create your own web application. This skill will help you build applications that can provide services to people such as e-commerce, social media apps, and more. It will also help you land a job as a software developer/engineer.

Instructions:

Information

  1. Navigate through each unit through the navigation bar by just clicking the unit that you would like to study. It is recommended that you study from Unit 1 to Unit 3 respectively.

  2. Quizzes are located at the end of a unit. Answer each quiz to test your knowledge, after hitting the "Submit" button, you will be shown your quiz results as well as the correct answers.

  3. Happy Coding!
